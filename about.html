Our Mission

Empowering small businesses and connecting communities through accessible micro-lending.

Our Story

Vendor Raise was born out of a need to help small business owners access capital more easily. Traditional lending institutions often overlook small vendors, making it difficult for them to grow. We saw an opportunity to connect these hardworking entrepreneurs with local lenders who want to support their community while earning fair returns.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every small business owner has access to the funding they need to thrive. By democratizing access to capital and fostering local economic connections, we believe we can create stronger, more resilient communities.

How We Help

Our platform simplifies the lending process for both vendors and lenders. Vendors can create profiles, submit funding requests, and get matched with lenders quickly. Lenders can browse vetted opportunities and support businesses they believe in.
